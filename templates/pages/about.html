TwitterBash is a fun little project built by Jonathan Fielding based on work by Gaurav Jassal

The core idea behind TwitterBash is to strip away the gui for twitter, what twitter does best is share 140 character long messages and what TwitterBash demonstrates is that it works perfectly well as a console tool
